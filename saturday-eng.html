Saturday

Needed sleep

Because I had not slept for at least 6 hours on any day of the week, today I allowed myself to sleep longer and woke up only after ten o'clock. When I woke up, I was of course still sleepy, and spent the first hour just sitting on the internet doing nothing productive.

Productive saturday

As the clock approached 12:00, I decided to find a quick snack in the fridge and start a new section on the Thinking Systematics course work. After spending less than an hour studying theory and then two hours writing my own work, I decided to take some time off from writing and go to the store to buy the products I needed, while resting my head in the fresh air. Coming back from the store, I prepare lunch for myself and after eating that, I finish the section on thinking systematics work, with which I finish this weeks homeworks.

Evening relaxation

This Saturday night, I decided to go with my friends to the "Vecais Pēteris" cafe next door. When we arrive at this cafe and order food, we started playing a variety of board games that were available to "Vecais Pēteris" clients. When food was brought to us, we put aside board games and started talking about a variety of topics. As soon as everyone eat their meals, we said goodbye and went to our home. Coming home, I decided to arrange my room and watch a good movie. After watching the movie, I went to bed.
