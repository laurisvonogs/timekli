Sunday

Boring morning

Sundays for me are my favorite days because every Sunday I can spend more time relaxing, due to the fact that all the homework is done. On Sundays, I usually get up after ten o'clock and the first two hours, like yesterday, are spent reading news on the Internet.

Work studies

Since all my homework for the university is done, I devote three hours om Sunday to improving my work knowledge. This Sunday I started learning Azure Databricks and in what situations it is best to use it. After that, I started learning about how to make the most of Azure resources, and what resources in which situations are most effective. Mostly I watched videos, where someone talks about these topics or I read official documentation on the topics I studied.

End of the week

As I finish learning about Azure resources, I prepare dinner for myself. As I eat dinner, I start thinking about next week's plans - what will be my goals for next week, what I have to do, what I want to do and how much time I can devote to learning. Although this week was not one of the most interesting of which I could tell, in terms of productivity, I did more this week than in previous weeks. I hope next week will be at least as productive as this week, if not more productive, because the more time I spend on training now, the easier the end of the session will be.
