At the end of the practical work, the next lecture continues with I.Bule, but in this lecture we continue to learn the theory of Mathematical Analysis.

Gym and homework

Leaving the seminar, I immediately go to the gym, where I train my muscles, and also rest my mind from school and work. After spending a little more than an hour in the gym, I go back to the dorm. When I arrive at the dorm, I eat lunch and devote 20 minutes to the internet. Then I start doing homework for mathematical analysis, which took about two hours. After completing my homework, I spend about 30 minutes resting, which I usually spend on social networks. After the evening routine, I go to bed early so I can sleep.
